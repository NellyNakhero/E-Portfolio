Hello Guys!!

My name is Linfeng Sun, an outgoing young individual who is a Chinese native from Shenzhen, China. I am currently studying linguistics and Media criticism courses intending to ensure I channel the needs of society and educate people about the English language and linguistics in general.

I enjoyed reading motivational books such as “Awaken The Giant Within” by Anthony Robbins and discovering my purpose in life. Aside from reading, I entertain myself through sports and playing video games. In addition, I am an expert basketball player, and I enjoy swimming during my leisure time.
